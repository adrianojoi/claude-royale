Claude Royale is a real-time multiplayer battler that runs entirely in your browser — no install, no account. Deploy troops, spells and champions, manage your elixir, and take down the enemy king in fast 1v1 duels. Built for phones held sideways, but plays anywhere.

▶ Play now: clauderoyale.net
💬 Join the community: Discord

Features

  • ⚔️ Real-time 1v1 with matchmaking, private rooms, live spectating and auto-reconnect
  • 🃏 49 collectible cards — shields, life-steal, healing auras, poison, flyers, spawner buildings, champions with active abilities, and card evolutions
  • 🤖 Train vs bots in 3 difficulties, or watch bot-vs-bot to study card interactions
  • 🏆 Trophies & leaderboard, deck builder, replays of every match
  • 📱 PWA — installable, works offline for menus, fullscreen landscape

How to play with a friend

Open the game in two tabs (or two phones) and press ⚔️ Battle in both — or use a private room code.
